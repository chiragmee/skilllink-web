## Full Description (Google Play — up to 4,000 chars)

```
SkillLink connects you with verified local experts for any skill you want to learn — sports coaching, music lessons, yoga, tech tutoring, cooking classes, and more.

Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to level up, SkillLink makes it easy to find the right expert, book a session, and pay securely — all in minutes.

🎯 FOR LEARNERS
• Browse 100+ skill categories: badminton, swimming, guitar, Python, yoga, dance & more
• Filter by location, price, mode (online/offline), and rating
• View verified expert profiles with reviews from real learners
• Book a session in under 2 minutes
• Pay securely via UPI — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm
• Your payment is held in escrow until the session is completed

⭐ FOR EXPERTS
• Turn your passion into income — set your own hourly rate
• Manage your schedule with a simple availability calendar
• Accept or decline booking requests
• Get paid directly to your bank account after each session
• Build your reputation with ratings and reviews

🔒 TRUST & SAFETY
• Expert verification badge for identity-checked coaches
• Escrow payment protection — money only released after session completion
• Transparent cancellation and refund policy
• In-app chat with your expert after booking
• 24/7 dispute resolution support

💳 TRANSPARENT PRICING
• No subscription. No hidden fees.
• Pay only when you book. Platform charges a small 15% service fee.
• Refunds processed automatically for cancellations per policy.

SkillLink is built for India — UPI-first payments, Hindi language support coming soon, and a growing network of verified coaches in every city.

Download free. Book your first session today.
```
